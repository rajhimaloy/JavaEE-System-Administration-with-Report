Introduction and Industry Coverage:

ABC Bank Limited is a commercial bank in Bangladesh registered under the Companies Act 1994. It was established on June 3, 2001. ABC Bank had 132 branches as of March,2019 .[3] Its head office is located at Hadi Mansion, 2, Dilkusha C/A, Dhaka-1000, Bangladesh. ABC Bank Ltd is a 3rd generation private commercial bank, was established by a group of local entrepreneurs. The bank, aside from conventional, also provides Islamic banking through designated branches. The Bank has real-time online banking branches (of both urban and rural areas) network to provide online banking.
